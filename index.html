Solar Para Sa Bayan

Republic Act No. 11357

Nature & Scope

Grant to construct, install, establish, operate, and maintain Distributed Energy Resources (DERs) and microgrids utilizing renewable energy technology to provide power in remote, unviable, unserved, or underserved areas.

Consumer Protection

  • Retail rates must be the "true cost" and approved by ERC (Sec. 8).
  • No government subsidy for rates (Sec. 8).
  • Must establish a consumer desk for complaints (Sec. 7).
  • Lifeline rates for marginalized end users (Sec. 8).

Responsibilities

Operate in the least-cost manner. Provide efficient and reliable service. Create employment opportunities, prioritizing residents of the franchise area (Sec. 17).

Microgrid

A group of interconnected loads and distributed energy resources within clearly defined electrical boundaries that acts as a single controllable entity with respect to the grid.

Unserved Area

An area with no electricity access, no distribution system lines, no solar PV home systems, or no connection to any microgrid.

Underserved Area

Areas where service is less than 24 hours daily, non-compliant with Distribution Code, or frequent interruptions (12+ times in 12 months).

Distributed Energy Resources (DERs)

Smaller power sources, demand-side or supply-side, aggregated to meet regular demand (e.g., renewable facilities, energy storage, EV charging).
